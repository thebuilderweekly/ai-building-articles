# The Builder Weekly Articles

Foundation articles on AI building, AI economics, and trust in AI systems.

Each article is a canonical reference answering a specific question. Maintained by The Builder Weekly editors with autonomous review by [@tbw-ai](https://github.com/tbw-ai) and continuous updates via community contributions.

Published at [thebuilderweekly.com/articles](https://thebuilderweekly.com/articles).

## What's in this repo

- `entries/` - individual article folders, one per article
- `schema/article.schema.json` - metadata schema every article must validate against
- `queue/topics.json` - topics in the pipeline for future articles
- `CONTRIBUTING.md` - how to contribute articles or edits
