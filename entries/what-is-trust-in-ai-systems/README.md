# What is trust in AI systems?

Trust pillar anchor. Reframes trust as an engineering problem, not a faith problem. Covers the four-layer trust stack (input, process, output, outcome verification), the distinction between testing and verification, the accountability gap in most agent frameworks, and why trust is becoming the competitive advantage in the agent-product market.

~2,400 words. References Vol XII (AI Alone Is Fragile) and the accountability-loop tutorial.
